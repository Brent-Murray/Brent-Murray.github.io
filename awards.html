  • Patrick David Campbell Graduate Fellowship, University of British Columbia
    Fellowships totalling $51,600 have been endowed by Patrick David Campbell, B.A.Sc. 1947 and the University of British Columbia. The awards are offered to graduate students in a master's or doctoral program and are made on the recommendation of the Faculty of Graduate and Postdoctoral Studies.
  • Elwyn Gregg Memorial Fellowship, University of British Columbia
    Fellowships totalling $34,300 have been endowed by a bequest from the Estate of Elwyn Gregg for graduate students in any field of study. Mr. Gregg believed that too often the best graduate talent from UBC may be lost to British Columbia and Canada because of financial attractions and facilities available beyond our borders. It was his hope that this fellowship may be used to attract such abilities to British Columbia. Prominent consideration is to be given to Masters and Ph.D. candidates in Medicine, Languages, Engineering and Forestry, but not necessarily in that order.

  • Marcia Anne Faurer/PeterBennet Memorial Scholarship in Geography, University of Winnipeg
